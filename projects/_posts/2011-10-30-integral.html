This is a human-friendly interface for the integration feature of Wolfram Alpha.

  • Limits of integration must be either all filled (for definite integrals) or all blank (for indefinite integrals).
  • You may use (theta), (rho), and (phi) to specify Greek letters anywhere except in the Differentials field.
  • Other functions and constants you can use: pi e infinity sqrt(x) log(x) sin(x) cos(x) tan(x)
  • Updated on April 22, 2013: Decreased error rate and allowed Greek letters to specified using (rho), etc.
    • {% comment %}
  • Because Alpha cannot parse Φ, all occurrences of Φ will automatically be replaced with f before submitting to Alpha.
