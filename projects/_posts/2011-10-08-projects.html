Duolingo Test Center

Senior Software Engineer
January 2014 - Present

Duolingo is a free alternative to Rosetta Stone. Available on web and mobile, it was named 2013 App of the Year by Apple. Its game mechanics are pretty addicting - I signed up on a whim and ended up completing five language courses.

Test Center is an ambitious attempt to replace overpriced language certification exams like the TOEFL and IELTS with Duolingo's own remotely proctored $20 exam.

I wrote much of Test Center's Python backend and the entirety of its JavaScript frontend.

Duolingo for Windows Phone

Senior Software Engineer
August 2014 - Present

I took on the task of single-handedly writing Duolingo's WP app as a side project while working on Test Center. It launched in November 2014.

Windows Central has a nice walkthrough with screenshots and a demo video.

Within a month of its release into the wild, the app reached #1 in the Education category with a 4.7 star rating based on over 5000 store reviews. Who knew there were so many Windows Phones out there?
